Who We Are

India's Road Journey Specialists

India Tours by Car & Driver is a Jaipur-based travel company founded with a single purpose: to make road travel across India seamless, safe and memorable.

We do not put you on a crowded bus or a train. We give you your own vehicle, your own driver, and the freedom to stop wherever the road calls you. Our Cars of premium SUVs — Innova Crysta, Kia Carens, Force Urbania, Tempo Travellers — are regularly serviced and maintained to the highest standards.

Every one of our drivers is selected through a rigorous process — commercial licence, police verification, local route knowledge, and English communication. They are not just drivers; they are guides, navigators and companions on your India journey.

Est. 2012 — Jaipur, Rajasthan
Our Journey

From Jaipur to All of India

2012
The Beginning

Started with 2 Innova vehicles and a team of 3, offering Rajasthan tours out of Jaipur. First 100 customers in year one.

2015
North India Expansion

Expanded operations to Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand and the Golden Triangle circuit. Cars grew to 15 vehicles.

2018
All India Coverage

Launched South India, Kerala and Northeast India packages. Established driver partners across 20+ states.

2024
15,000 Travellers Milestone

Crossed 15,000 happy travellers. Introduced Force Urbania and Tempo Traveller for group tours.
