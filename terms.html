Itinerary Modifications

We understand that travel plans change. We aim to be as flexible as possible subject to driver and vehicle availability.

  • Date changes requested more than 7 days in advance are accommodated at no extra charge (subject to availability).
  • Route extensions or additional drop locations added mid-tour will be charged at our standard per-km rate.
  • Changes to vehicle type are subject to availability and any price difference will be invoiced accordingly.
  • We reserve the right to substitute an equivalent vehicle if the booked car becomes unavailable due to breakdown or emergency.

Driver & Vehicle Conduct

All drivers are professionally licensed, locally experienced, and background-verified. Vehicles are regularly maintained and insured as per Indian Motor Vehicle Act requirements.

  • The driver has full authority over the vehicle's operation including decisions on road safety, route and driving hours.
  • Smoking, consumption of alcohol, and behaviour that disturbs the driver are strictly prohibited inside the vehicle.
  • Drivers will follow applicable driving hour limits (max 10 hrs/day) for safety. Early/late requests beyond these hours may be declined.
  • Any damage to the vehicle caused by passengers (beyond normal wear) will be chargeable to the client.
