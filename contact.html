FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions before you book.

What's included in the tour price?
Our prices include a private air-conditioned car, an experienced English-speaking driver, fuel, all toll taxes, state entry taxes, and driver night halt charges. Hotels, meals, monument entry fees, and guide fees are not included unless specifically quoted.
Do you offer airport pickup and drop?
Yes, airport transfers are available 24/7 from all major Indian airports including Delhi (IGI), Jaipur (JAI), Mumbai (BOM), and others. Simply mention your flight details when enquiring and we'll arrange a driver to meet you at arrivals.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 7–10 days in advance, especially during peak season (October to February). For last-minute travel, we can often accommodate requests within 24–48 hours depending on vehicle availability.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations 7+ days before the tour are fully refundable. Cancellations 3–7 days before incur a 25% charge. Cancellations within 48 hours are charged at 50%, and no-shows are non-refundable. Please see our full Terms & Conditions for details.
Can I customise my itinerary?
Absolutely — all our tours are fully customisable. Tell us your starting point, destinations, travel dates, and budget, and we'll build a personalised itinerary. There's no extra charge for custom planning.
Are your drivers English-speaking?
Yes — all our drivers speak conversational English and are knowledgeable about the destinations on your route. Many are well-versed in local history, restaurants, and hidden gems. They're more than just drivers; they're your local guide.
Do you cover all of India?
Yes! We operate pan-India. Our most popular routes are in Rajasthan, the Golden Triangle (Delhi–Agra–Jaipur), Kerala, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Goa, and South India. We can arrange tours from any major city.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers (NEFT/IMPS), UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and cash. International clients can pay via wire transfer. All prices are quoted in INR. A 25% advance may be required for peak season or multi-day tours.
